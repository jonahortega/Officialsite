## Problem Statement

College students today face a fragmented ecosystem for discovering and participating in campus events:

1. **Discovery Problem**: Events are scattered across Instagram, Facebook, email lists, flyers, and word-of-mouth
2. **Ticketing Problem**: Organizations struggle with manual RSVP tracking, payment processing, and door management
3. **Engagement Problem**: No centralized platform connecting students to their campus organizations
4. **Trust Problem**: Students don't know which events are legitimate, safe, and worth attending

**Current Solutions Fall Short:**
- **Instagram/Facebook**: Social media platforms not built for events or ticketing
- **Shotgun**: Event ticketing only, no community features
- **Doorlist/Engage**: Founder teams removed from campus, lack authentic student connections
- **University Apps**: Limited to specific schools, poor user experience

---

## Solution

Greek Life App is a mobile-first platform that solves these problems by providing:

### Core Features

1. **Event Discovery**: Beautiful, searchable feed of all events happening at a user's university
2. **QR Code Ticketing**: Seamless ticket purchase and validation system
3. **Organization Profiles**: Complete profiles for fraternities, sororities, and clubs
4. **Payment Processing**: Integrated payment system with 5% commission model
5. **Map Integration**: Visual event locations and navigation
6. **Link Attachments**: Organizations can attach application forms, external links, and resources to events (similar to Engage)
7. **Event Analytics**: Organizations can track attendance, revenue, and engagement

### Our Unique Value Proposition

1. **Campus-Native Founders**: We're current students with direct connections and credibility
2. **Greek Life Focus**: Purpose-built for fraternities, sororities, and campus organizations
3. **Authentic Market Access**: Friends in leadership positions at target universities
4. **Polished UI/UX**: Inspired by Doorlist's design quality, built for our specific market

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

#### Primary: Ticket Commission (5%)
- 5% commission on all paid event ticket sales
- Automated payment splitting (95% to organization, 5% to us)
- Primary revenue driver

**Revenue Calculation Example:**
- 5,000 active users (Year 1)
- Average user attends 2 paid events per year
- Average ticket price: $20
- Total ticket sales: 5,000 users × 2 events × $20 = **$200,000**
- Our commission (5%): **$10,000**

#### Future Revenue Streams (Year 2+)

1. **Premium Organization Features**: $5-10/month per organization
   - Advanced analytics
   - Priority event promotion
   - Custom branding

2. **Sponsored Events**: $100-500 per event
   - Featured placement
   - Banner advertising

3. **Enterprise University Licensing**: $5,000-10,000/year per university

   - University-wide integration

## Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Primary Risks

#### 1. Competitive Threat from Doorlist
**Risk Level: HIGH**

**Risk**: Doorlist has more funding, stronger brand, and could become dominant before we launch.

**Mitigation:**
- Speed to market: Launch September 2026
- Focus on authentic campus connections they cannot replicate
- Specialize in Greek life niche
- Build strong ambassador network as competitive moat
- Leverage student-founder advantage

#### 2. Incorrect Fund Allocation
**Risk Level: MEDIUM**

**Risk**: Mispending funds on ineffective events or weak ambassadors.

**Mitigation:**
- Clear ambassador selection criteria and vetting process
- Performance-based compensation
- Start with smaller events to validate approach
- Regular budget reviews and adjustments
- Learn from early mistakes, iterate quickly

#### 3. Technical Issues / App Errors
**Risk Level: MEDIUM**

**Risk**: App crashes, payment failures, or poor user experience could damage reputation.

**Mitigation:**
- Thorough testing phase before launch
- Beta testing with real organizations
- Hire experienced engineer
- Monitor app performance continuously
- Quick response to technical issues
- Have backup plans for critical features

#### 4. Low User Adoption
**Risk Level: MEDIUM**

**Risk**: Students don't download or use the app despite marketing efforts.

**Mitigation:**
- Strong ambassador program for authentic promotion
- Launch events to demonstrate value
- Guarantee event lineup to build initial content
- Focus on organizations first, then students
- Word-of-mouth through Greek life networks

#### 5. Ambassador Program Failure
**Risk Level: MEDIUM**

**Risk**: Ambassadors don't perform, waste money, or damage brand.

**Mitigation:**
- Careful selection process
- Clear contracts and expectations
- Performance-based compensation
- Regular check-ins and support
- Ability to replace underperforming ambassadors

#### 6. Regulatory / Legal Issues
**Risk Level: LOW**

**Risk**: University policies, payment regulations, or data privacy issues.

**Mitigation:**
- Legal consultation before launch
- Compliance with FERPA, GDPR
- Clear terms of service and privacy policy
- Work with universities, not against them

### Risk Prioritization

**Immediate Focus:**
1. Competitive threat (Doorlist)
2. Technical execution
3. Ambassador program success

**Ongoing Monitoring:**
4. User adoption metrics
5. Fund allocation efficiency
6. Regulatory compliance
